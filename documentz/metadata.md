## Full Description

Notify2u is a minimalist, privacy-focused daily planner designed to help you stay on top of your tasks and financial obligations. With its beautiful Sky Blue and Pink theme, it brings a fresh and calming perspective to productivity.

**Features:**

- **Payment Reminders:** Easily track bills, rent, and subscriptions. Never miss a due date again.
- **To-Do List:** Organize your daily tasks with categories and priority levels.
- **Calendar View:** Visualize your month and see upcoming activities at a glance.
- **Local & Secure:** Your data never leaves your device. No cloud, no tracking, total privacy.
- **Quick Add:** Use the persistent notification to quickly jot down tasks without opening the app.
- **Home Screen Widget:** Keep your top tasks visible on your home screen.

Designed with Material 3 and built for speed, Notify2u is the ultimate companion for your busy life.
