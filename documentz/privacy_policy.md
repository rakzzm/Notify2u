# Privacy Policy for Notify2u

**Last Updated: October 2023**

Notify2u ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the App.

### 1. Data Collection

We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information to external servers. All data you enter into the App (tasks, reminders, settings) is stored locally on your device in a secure Room database.

### 2. Permissions

The App requests the following permissions to function correctly:

- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS:** To send you reminders for upcoming tasks and payments.
- **SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM:** To ensure reminders are delivered precisely at the time you set.

### 3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics, or advertising networks that collect your data.

### 4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on the security of your device. We recommend using device-level encryption and passcodes.

### 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on our website.

### 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
